Youssou N'Dour

Biography

Youssou N'Dour is a Senegalese singer, songwriter, composer, occasional actor, businessman, and politician. He is one of the most celebrated African musicians in history and is known for his extraordinary vocal range and for introducing the world to Mbalax, a popular Senegalese music genre.
