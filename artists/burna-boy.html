Burna Boy

Biography

Burna Boy, born Damini Ebunoluwa Ogulu, is a Nigerian singer, songwriter, and record producer. Known as the "African Giant," he gained international fame with his unique fusion of Afrobeat, dancehall, reggae, and pop.
