Angélique Kidjo

Biography

Angélique Kidjo is a Beninese singer, songwriter, actress, and activist, celebrated as one of Africa's most iconic and influential artists. Known for her unique blend of African music with elements of jazz, funk, and world music, she has won multiple Grammy Awards and is a strong advocate for education and humanitarian causes.
