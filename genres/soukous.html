Soukous

What is Soukous?

Soukous is a dance music genre that originated in the Congo region of Africa. It is known for its lively rhythms, melodic guitar solos, and fast-paced beats that encourage energetic dancing.

History and Key Characteristics

Soukous evolved from the Congolese rumba in the 1940s and gained popularity across Africa in the 1960s. Its defining features include intricate guitar riffs, rapid tempos, and call-and-response vocal styles. The genre is often played during celebrations and parties due to its vibrant and uplifting energy.

Prominent Artists

  • Tabu Ley Rochereau
