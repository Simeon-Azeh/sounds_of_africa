Afrobeats

What is Afrobeats?

Afrobeats is a popular music genre originating from West Africa, particularly Nigeria and Ghana. It blends traditional African rhythms with elements of hip-hop, funk, and dancehall. Known for its upbeat tempo and infectious melodies, Afrobeats has gained global recognition in recent years.

History and Key Characteristics

The genre emerged in the 2000s, heavily influenced by earlier styles such as Afrobeat (popularized by Fela Kuti). Its distinguishing features include polyrhythms, percussive beats, and vibrant vocal styles that often touch on themes of love, politics, and celebration.
