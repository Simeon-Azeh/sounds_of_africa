Highlife

What is Highlife?

Highlife is a popular music genre that originated in Ghana and Nigeria in the early 20th century. It is characterized by its use of Western instruments like guitars, trumpets, and saxophones blended with African rhythms and melodies.

History and Key Characteristics

Highlife developed in the 1920s and gained widespread popularity during the independence movements of West Africa. Its unique sound combines traditional Akan music, jazz, and Afro-Cuban influences. Highlife is known for its lilting guitar lines and vibrant horn sections that create a joyful and uplifting vibe.

Prominent Artists

  • E.T. Mensah
  • Osibisa
  • Rex Lawson
